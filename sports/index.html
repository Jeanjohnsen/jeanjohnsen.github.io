Still useful today

  • Respect the process, not just the headline result.
  • Keep feedback loops short and honest.
  • Build for execution when tired, not only when fresh.
  • Stay calm enough to make the next useful decision.
Good systems are not loud. They are well-timed, well-practised and dependable when people are under pressure.
